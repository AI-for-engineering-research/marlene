Summer Research Progress

Results, visualizations, and milestones from some of the summer research explorations for my multi-sensor contrail observation project.

Detecting contrails in VIIRS imagery

Testing whether an existing contrail detection model trained on GOES ABI imagery can be applied to VIIRS data with only little tuning. VIIRS offers higher spatial resolution (375 m - 750 m) but slightly different spectral channels, requiring a band-mapping adapter before inference.

WindBorne radiosonde data

Investigating the data products of the WindBorne API and the potential of using the data for our region of interest (CONUS). First steps will be familiarization with accessing the data an checking availability of measurements over selected locations.
